House Price Prediction

House price prediction is a common application of machine learning. It involves analyzing various features of a house, such as the number of bedrooms, size of the house, presence of a basement, etc., to predict its price.

Logistic Regression

Logistic regression is a statistical method used for binary classification problems. It predicts the probability of occurrence of an event by fitting data to a logistic function. Despite its name, it is used for classification, not regression.
